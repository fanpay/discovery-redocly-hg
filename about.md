# About this portal

The Payment Services Developer Portal is the central resource for developers integrating with our payment platform. It covers authentication, sandbox testing, full API reference, and end-to-end integration walkthroughs.

## What we support

- **REST API** — JSON over HTTPS, standard HTTP verbs, OAuth 2.0 authentication.
- **Sandbox environment** — A fully isolated sandbox for development and testing without affecting live transactions.
- **Multiple payment methods** — Credit and debit cards, ACH bank transfers, and securely vaulted tokens.
- **Recurring billing** — Subscription management with configurable frequencies, amounts, and schedules.
- **Hosted payment pages** — Generate payment links for remote and asynchronous collection flows.
- **Customer vault** — Store customer profiles and payment methods securely for future transactions.

## Who this is for

This portal is intended for developers and technical teams building integrations against the Payment Services API — whether that's a custom checkout, a back-office billing system, or a partner platform.

## Documentation feedback

If you find an error or gap in the documentation, reach out to your integration contact or submit feedback through your internal support channel.
