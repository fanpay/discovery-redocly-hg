Whether you're accepting payments through a portal, embedding payments into your application, or building custom workflows, Merchant Services provides the tools to scale with your business.

## 🧠 What this platform enables

Merchant Services helps you:

- Accept payments across in-person, online, and integrated channels  
- Streamline payment operations and reduce manual effort  
- Build custom payment experiences using APIs  
- Scale with reliable, production-ready infrastructure  

---

## 🏗️ How it works

- **Customer / Checkout** — Payment is initiated  
- **Payment API** — Processes and routes the transaction  
- **Processor** — Executes the payment  
- **Webhook** — Sends real-time updates back to your system

## 🔑 Key capabilities

**Integrated payments**  
Connect payments directly to your existing business systems.

**Recurring billing**  
Automate subscriptions and scheduled payments.

**Virtual terminal**  
Accept payments anywhere using a secure, browser-based interface.

**Hosted payment forms**  
Create branded checkout experiences without building from scratch.

**Digital wallets**  
Accept Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, and Venmo.

**API-first integration**  
Build fully custom payment experiences with complete control.

**Real-time webhooks**  
Receive instant updates for payment events and status changes.
