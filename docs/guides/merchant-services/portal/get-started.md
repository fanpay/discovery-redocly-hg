# Sample Payments Platform Get Started

This guide walks you through accessing your account and configuring essential settings to begin using the Sample Payments Platform.

---

## Accessing your account

Follow these steps to access your account:

1. **Request your login**  
   If you have not received login credentials, contact your account manager or support team to request access.

2. **Activate your account**  
   Once credentials are received, activate your account within 7 days of when they were sent.

3. **Log in**  
   After activation, log in to the Sample Payments Platform web portal using the provided URL and your credentials.

> **Note:**  
> If you do not have a Merchant Account, visit the Merchant Services Get Started page and contact us to begin.

---

## Prerequisites and configuration

Before using the platform, ensure your account is properly configured:

### Payment types
Confirm your account is enabled for the payment types you need (for example, credit cards or ACH).

### ACH payments
If you plan to accept ACH payments, your account must be configured accordingly. This may require contacting support or your account manager.

### User management
If you have administrative access, you can:
- Create user accounts
- Assign roles
- Configure permissions for your team

### General settings
Update your merchant account settings, including:
- Fraud tools
- Custom receipts
- Sale item management

---

## Common use cases

The Sample Payments Platform supports a variety of workflows, including:

- **Virtual terminal** — Accept payments directly from your browser  
- **Online forms** — Create branded payment forms  
- **Payment links** — Send payment requests to customers  
- **Recurring payments** — Manage subscription-based billing
