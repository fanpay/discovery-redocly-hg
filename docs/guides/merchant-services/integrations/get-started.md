# Online Payment Integrations Get Started

## Prerequisites

Welcome to the Online Payment Integrations Guide! This guide provides detailed information on how to seamlessly integrate various online payment solutions into your applications. Our platform supports a variety of integration options tailored to meet diverse business requirements, including Embedded Payments, Digital Wallets, Hosted Payment Forms, Mobile SDKs, API integrations, and Webhook notifications.

Before beginning the integration, ensure you have the following:

- **A Merchant Account:** You will need an active Merchant Account.
- **Access Tokens and Secret Key:** To integrate with online payment solutions, you'll need an Access Token and Secret Key for authentication and configuration of your payment panel. Our Integration Team securely provides these credentials.
- **Access to Developer Portal:** The portal provides additional resources, API documentation, and integration tools.
- **Knowledge of JWT (JSON Web Token) Creation:** You'll need to create signed JWTs for authenticating transactions. A basic understanding of JWTs is recommended, as they are essential for securely passing data to the Embedded Payments SDK.

> **Note:** If you don't have a Merchant Account or Access Tokens and Secret Key, simply visit Merchant Services Get Started and contact us today.

## Integrated Payment Solutions

### Embedded Payments

Our embedded payments solution enables integrators to seamlessly add flexible payment capabilities directly into their website or mobile app. The key purpose is to provide a smooth, customized checkout experience for customers without redirecting them elsewhere to complete payment. With our JavaScript SDK, merchants can embed our payment interface into their site with just a few lines of code. This allows collecting payments across multiple methods including cards, digital wallets, and more.

### Digital Wallets

Digital wallets can provide merchants with an optimized checkout flow by allowing customers a quick and easy online payment option, and a lowered risk of fraudulent transactions. The Sample Payments Platform supports integrations for various digital wallets, including Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, Venmo, and Paze. Our expert team and integration resources make the process a breeze for online transactions.

- Digital Wallets Get Started

### Hosted Payment Form

The Hosted Payment Form (HPF) offers a quick and secure way to handle payments without directly managing sensitive data, thus reducing PCI compliance scope. It is ideal for businesses that need a straightforward integration with minimal development effort. The HPF ensures that card details are handled securely through an iFrame, keeping your system out of PCI scope.
