# Embedded Payments Overview

## Embedded Payments Integration for Web and Mobile

Embedded Payments lets you integrate secure, flexible payment capabilities directly into your website or mobile app. Customers complete checkout within your experience with no redirects, no friction, delivering a smooth, customized checkout flow that keeps users engaged.

## Why Embedded Payments

Embedded Payments is designed for businesses that want full control over the checkout experience while minimizing PCI compliance requirements. By leveraging platform-hosted iFrames, sensitive card data never touches your servers, reducing risk and compliance overhead.

**Benefits:**

- **Seamless User Experience:** Customers stay on your site or app during checkout.
- **Multiple Payment Methods:** Support for credit cards and digital wallets.
- **Reduced PCI Scope:** The platform handles sensitive data securely.
- **Customizable Design:** Match the payment interface to your brand.
- **Modern Checkout Options:** Enable Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, Venmo, and Paze.

## Integration Highlights

Integration is powered by our JavaScript SDK, which makes implementation simple and flexible. Add the SDK to your site, render the payment interface inside a container, and customize its appearance to match your brand.

**Integration Steps:**

1. **Install JavaScript SDK:** Include the SDK in your web or mobile app.
2. **Render Payment UI:** Use a DIV container to embed the payment interface.
3. **Customize Design:** Configure colors, panels, and layout for brand consistency.
4. **Enable Digital Wallets:** Activate Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, Venmo, and Paze.
5. **Display Products:** Show purchased items for confirmation before payment.

## Key Capabilities

- **Flexible Integration:** Embed the payment UI anywhere using a DIV container.
- **PCI Compliance:** Card data is collected through platform-hosted iFrames.
- **Customizable Design:** Configure colors, panels, and layout to match your site.
- **Digital Wallet Support:** Offer Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal & Venmo, and Paze.
- **Product Display:** Show purchased items for confirmation before payment.

## Considerations

- **Security:** Ensure proper configuration for PCI compliance.
- **Browser Compatibility:** Verify SDK support across major browsers.
- **Digital Wallet Setup:** Requires additional configuration for Apple Pay and Google Pay.
