# Run Your Business

Operational guides for accepting payments and managing customers — without writing code.

## What do you want to do?

- [Accept Payments](./accept-payments/index.md) — Collect card and bank payments from customers
- [Manage Customers](./manage-customers/index.md) — View and update customer records

## Frequently asked questions

### Do I need technical knowledge to use these guides?

No. These guides are written for business users. Where API calls are shown, they are illustrative — your integration team handles the technical setup.

### How do I access the sandbox for testing?

See [Sandbox](../global/sandbox.md). Your integration team provides sandbox credentials.

### Who do I contact if a payment fails or a customer is not found?

See [Support / Help](../global/support.md) for escalation paths and support contacts.
