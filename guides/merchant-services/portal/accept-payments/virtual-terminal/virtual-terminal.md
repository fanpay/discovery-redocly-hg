# Virtual Terminal

## Overview

Welcome to the Sample Payments Virtual Terminal.

The Virtual Terminal provides a secure, streamlined way to accept and manage payments without requiring any system integration. Whether you need to process transactions quickly, support tax-exempt customers, or simplify payment collection, the Virtual Terminal enables you to start accepting payments immediately.

Sample Payments is **Level 1 PCI-DSS compliant**, helping reduce compliance burden for your business.

With this non-integrated payment solution, you can begin processing transactions as soon as you access the portal—no complex setup or development work required.

---

## Key features

The Virtual Terminal is designed to simplify day-to-day payment operations:

- **Accept payments**  
  Process credit card, ACH, and batch transactions with ease

- **Refunds and recurring payments**  
  Issue refunds and schedule recurring payments directly from the portal

- **Customer record management**  
  Store customer information securely for faster future transactions

- **Tax-exempt processing**  
  Support tax-exempt transactions for eligible customers

- **Payment links**  
  Capture key transaction details and generate payment links for easy collection

---

## Configuration

You can begin accepting payments immediately after accessing the portal. However, some features require additional configuration:

### Required configurations

- **ACH payments**  
  Enable ACH processing for your merchant account (may require contacting support or your account manager)

- **Custom fields**  
  Configure custom fields to capture additional transaction data

---

## Transaction management

The Virtual Terminal provides tools to manage your payment activity efficiently:

- **Issue refunds** for completed transactions  
- **Cancel or prevent unprocessed transactions** to avoid unnecessary fees  
- **Batch and settle transactions** to accelerate deposits  
- **Capture customer details during payment processing** for recordkeeping and reuse  

---

## Use cases

The Virtual Terminal supports a variety of common payment workflows:

- **Accept one-time payments**
- **Set up recurring payments**
- **Create and share payment links**
