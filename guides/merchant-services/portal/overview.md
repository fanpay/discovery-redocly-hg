# Sample Payments Platform Overview

The Sample Payments Platform is a comprehensive solution designed to enhance digital payment experiences for businesses.

This section is intended for merchants and teams that want a portal-based experience for managing payment activity, reviewing resources, and working with platform payment capabilities—without needing to build a fully custom integration upfront.

---

## What this section helps you do

Use the Sample Payments Platform guidance to:

- Understand the platform experience available to merchants
- Review supporting resources in the Developer Portal
- Learn how the platform fits alongside Merchant Services APIs and integrations
- Identify the right path for your business based on your implementation needs

---

## When to use the platform

The platform experience is a strong fit when you want to:

- Start quickly with payment capabilities
- Manage payment workflows through a portal-based experience
- Reduce implementation complexity for business users
- Combine operational tools with a broader Merchant Services relationship
