# Online Payment Integrations Overview

## Build Seamless Checkout Experiences

Modern applications need secure, flexible payment solutions that work across web and mobile. Our integration options give you the tools to create frictionless checkout experiences, reduce compliance overhead, and support multiple payment methods, all with developer-friendly APIs and SDKs.

- **Embed Payments:** Keep customers in your app with a fully customizable payment interface.
- **Digital Wallets:** Enable Digital Wallets Offer Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, Venmo, and Paze for fast, secure checkout.
- **Hosted Payment Form:** Simplify compliance with a secure, hosted page that requires minimal coding.
- **Mobile SDK:** Add payment processing to your mobile app with lightweight SDKs.
- **API Integration:** Build advanced payment flows using robust APIs for full control.
- **Webhook Notifications:** Receive real-time notifications for payment events and status changes.

## Why Developers Choose Our Platform

Our payment integrations are designed for speed, security, and flexibility. You can:

- Support multiple payment methods without complex coding.
- Reduce PCI scope with hosted components.
- Deliver consistent experiences across web and mobile.
